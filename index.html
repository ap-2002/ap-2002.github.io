About Me

I'm Aakash Prasad, and I am currently a student at South Brunswick High School in New Jersey. I am a member of one of our High School's robotics teams, 750B, and we compete in the VEX Robotics Competetion. I serve as an all purpose member with experience in building and coding, while also participating in any community outreach we do. I have used Autodesk programs like Fusion 360 and Inventor 2019 to allow our team to plan changes to the robot to ensure smooth iterations. As a computer science student, I know and am learning several different areas of the topic and this includes languages. I have experience in Javascript, Python, Java, C, C++, and web development.
